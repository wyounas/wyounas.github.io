Welcome to my blog :)

This is my little corner of the internet, where I talk about writing, technology, model checking and formal methods, and other topics that interest me.

Sometimes, I write about technical topics to understand them better. In other words, I’m just trying to follow Confucius’ advice: “I hear and I forget. I see and I remember. I do and I understand.”

I’ve spent much of my career helping build and scale software systems, both as a founder and an engineer at companies like HubSpot and Customer.io. I’m fascinated by how large systems work–and fail. I enjoy learning about distributed systems, formal verification, and the craft of reliable and good quality software.
